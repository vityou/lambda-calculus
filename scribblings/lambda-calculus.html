1 Introduction

lambda-calculus is a language for writing lambda calculus programs. Is is not pure lambda calculus but you can choose to use only the pure features. It can also be used in the racket repl with racket -I lambda-calculus.

2 Guide

The language offers a limited set of forms.

The lambda expression, also known as an abstraction in lambda calculus, has the form

(lambda (arg-id) body)

or

(λ (arg-id) body)
